Cancellation Policy

We ask for at least 72 hour notice (phone, text, direct message or Fresha app), as a courtesy, if you will need to reschedule your appointment. This allows us enough time to fill your appointment and give other clients an opportunity to get on the schedule.

Your card won't be charged if you cancel up to 48 hours before the appointment. Otherwise you will be charged 50% of the service price for late cancellation, and 100% for not showing up.
